GENERAL INFORMATION

UPCI YOUTH MINISTRIES

BIBLE QUIZZING

The UPCI Youth Ministries is proud to sponsor two levels of quizzing. The Intermediate Division is open to 12- to 14-year-olds and the Experienced Division involves 15- to 18-year-olds. In addition to this, the Youth Ministries produces several quizzing resources that assist teams in preparing for competitions on the district and national level. For Bible Quiz Extravaganza, North American Bible Quiz Tournament, and resource information visit the Bible Quizzing website www.seniorbiblequizzing.com.

YOUTH MINSTRY TRAINING EVENT (YMTE)

The Youth Ministry Training Event (formerly known as The Commune-ity Meeting) is a conference geared toward empowering youth pastors and youth workers with biblically sound and relevant teaching. This youth ministry conference inspires, informs, and equips student workers with instruction and resources to impact their local ministries. For more information visit www.TheCommune-ity.com.

THE COMMUNE-ITY

A website geared toward empowering student pastors and student workers with relevant ministry resources. There are over 1,760 resources available! Join www.TheCommune-ity.com today. The best part...membership is FREE!

HYPHEN YOUNG ADULTS

Hyphen is the ministry of the YM that focuses on single individuals between ages 18–30. Hyphen seeks to bridge the gap between student ministry and adult programming. Our mission is to connect Young Adults to SERVICE . . . with PURPOSE . . . through RESOURCES . . . for a MISSION. To get started visit www.hyphenonline.org.

INSIDEOUT WEBZINE

Pentecostal teens can read about culturally relevant issues from a biblical perspective in this webzine produced exclusively by UPCI Youth Ministries. INSIDEOUT features theme-based articles, book and music reviews, interviews, student ministry profiles, and much more! Visit our www.insideoutmag.org.

YOUTH WEEK

We encourage every church to set aside a week in February for special emphasis on their youth. Material is published every year and is available on the YM website (UPCIyouth.com) for free. Lessons and resources are made possible through a partnership with the YM, PPH and Link247.

SHEAVES FOR CHRIST

Sheaves for Christ is the fundraising ministry of the UPCI Youth Ministries. Since its inception in 1952, almost $130 million has been raised to fund missions and ministry around the world. Visit www.sheavesforchrist.com for more information and downloadable resources.

CAMPUS MINISTRY INTERNATIONAL

Campus Ministry International (CMI) was formed to enable and encourage local churches to effectively reach out to the college and university populations in their vicinities with the gospel of Jesus Christ. This is done by a variety of means, including motivating districts and churches to establish and maintain campus evangelism, and provide materials and training to aid in the formation of these campus ministries. Get connected now at the CMI website www.campusministryonline.com.

APOSTOLIC YOUTH CORPS

Apostolic Youth Corps (AYC) facilitates the pairing of ministry-minded Apostolic youth with North American and international sites that need and/or desire evangelism assistance. AYC provides evangelism opportunities for various durations ranging from 7–11 days. For information about current trips visit the Apostolic Youth Corps website www.apostolicyouthcorps.com.

DEVOTE 365 & PRAYER INITIATIVES

Devote 365/2.0 are daily, yearlong devotionals that were produced by the UPCI Youth Ministries, and are intended to guide the reader toward a closer relationship with God with structured daily Scripture readings accompanied by rich, insightful commentary by youth ministers across the nation. The UPCI YM works with the World Network of Prayer to sponsor and support prayer resources and information for youth and young adults. For more information visit www.prayingyouth.com

NORTH AMERICAN YOUTH CONGRESS

North American Youth Congress (NAYC) is the premier youth conference of the United Pentecostal Church International. Thousands of young people attend this meeting and are exposed to inspiring preaching, teaching, singing, and great fellowship! NAYC takes place biennially in a city in North America and is anticipated with much excitement by young people and youth workers of the UPCI. For further details visit our website www.northamericanyouthcongress.com.

PROJECT 7 BIBLE CLUBS

Project 7 (P7) is a free resource for students to utilize for the purpose of starting Bible Clubs on their High School and Middle School campuses. P7 Clubs are student-led and student-driven opportunities for students to participate in a spiritually inspired, relationship-oriented, community-serving project in their school. If you are interested in starting or joining a P7 Club, make the pledge to be a P7 missionary. Check out P7clubs.com today!
