IN CASE OF EMERGENCY

If an emergency (medical or otherwise) occurs in America’s Center, call 314.342.5081. America’s Center will call 911 and direct emergency responders to the appropriate location.

In case of an emergency anywhere else, call 911 immediately. Be prepared to give your address or describe your location and the emergency. If the situation involves NAYC participants, staff, or guests, call the Conference Office at 314.342.5500 once the emergency has been stabilized.

DOME ACCESS

THERE IS ABSOLUTELY NO SAVING OF SEATS.

ADA ENTRANCE

• Broadway Central – located off of Broadway across from Baer Plaza

• Courtyard North Elevators – these are in the Convention Center just to the North of the Courtyard and to the South of the Annex/Hall 5

• Hearing impaired seating is available in Concourse Low, Section 107.

SECURITY CHECKPOINTS (INCLUDING METAL DETECTORS) ARE PLACED AT EACH STADIUM ENTRANCE.

• All bags will be searched. Please do not bring backpacks or large handbags, to ensure more efficient entry into the stadium. It is recommended that ladies limit purses to small clutch types.

• Respect the requests of all security staff in the stadium and hotels.

• Please maintain proper etiquette in the stadium, hotels, and restaurants. Remember to tip generously!

• Be careful crossing the streets and obey the traffic officers.

STROLLERS

Please check in strollers at Concourse Level 117 near the guest services center. Umbrella strollers are encouraged for quick entrance into the stadium.
